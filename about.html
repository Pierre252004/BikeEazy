Our Mission

At Bike Eazy, our mission is to empower every rider by making biking simple, accessible, and sustainable. Whether you're a daily commuter or a weekend explorer, we provide the tools, knowledge, and welcoming space to build, customize, and repair bikes with confidence. Rooted in Ottawa’s cycling community, we’re committed to promoting eco-friendly transportation and supporting a healthier, more connected city "one ride at a time".
